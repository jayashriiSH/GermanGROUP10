DAY 1

Berlin

Begin your journey in Germany’s vibrant capital, where historical grandeur meets peaceful natural retreats. Stroll through the vast Tiergarten, unwind by the lakes of Grunewald Forest, or explore Tempelhofer Feld — a former airport turned into an open green expanse. Berlin offers a refreshing introduction to urban nature, inviting travelers to slow down and observe the quiet harmony within a bustling city.

DAY 2

DRESDEN

Explore forested trails and sandstone cliffs near the Elbe River. Nature and architecture weave a story of contrasts. Time your steps to the quiet pulse of the landscape.

Munich

Neuschwanstein

Ascend wooded paths to the castle in the clouds. Each turn reveals storybook views and silence. A journey where magic lives in the trail itself..

DAY 5

Heidelberg

Walk the Philosopher’s Way for sweeping views of the city and castle. This elevated path encourages quiet reflection with every step. Heidelberg offers a peaceful interlude of thought and beauty.

DAY 6

Cologne

Wander along the Rhine or find peace in the city’s hidden green pockets. Cologne blends Gothic history with modern calm and riverside paths. A perfect place to pause, reflect, and breathe between adventures.

DAY 7

Frankfurt
