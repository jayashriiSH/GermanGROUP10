Best Airport to Fly into Germany

Looking for the cheapest airport? Berlin and Frankfurt are often more affordable than Munich. But your choice should also depend on your itinerary:

  • Frankfurt: Ideal for exploring castles, Rhine Valley, Black Forest, and wine regions.
  • Munich: Perfect for Bavarian culture—Lederhosen, sausages, Alps.
  • Berlin: Great base to explore Northern Germany cities.

Open Jaw Flights for Efficiency

Flying into one city and out of another can save time, though it may cost more and have rental car return fees. For a 1-week Germany itinerary, consider flying into Munich and leaving from Frankfurt.

Germany Itinerary - 7 Days

There’s no one-size-fits-all itinerary, but this structure offers a great starting point for exploring Germany in 7 days.

  • Day 1 - Munich: Arrive and explore Marienplatz, Viktualienmarkt, and the English Garden.
  • Day 2 - Neuschwanstein Castle Day Trip: Take a day trip to Füssen to see the fairy-tale castle.
  • Day 3 - Travel to Nuremberg: Visit Nuremberg Castle, the old town, and local bratwurst spots.
  • Day 4 - Berlin: Travel to Berlin, explore the Berlin Wall, Brandenburg Gate, and Museum Island.
  • Day 5 - Berlin Continued: Visit Checkpoint Charlie, East Side Gallery, and enjoy Berlin nightlife.
  • Day 6 - Travel to Frankfurt: Walk along the River Main, visit Römerberg and explore shopping streets.
  • Day 7 - Rhine Valley Day Trip: Cruise along the Rhine, see vineyards and medieval castles, and depart.
